# LinkedIn Photo Integration

## Instructions to Add Your Photo

1. **Download your LinkedIn profile photo:**
   - Go to your LinkedIn profile: https://www.linkedin.com/in/christophe-trefois/
   - Click on your profile photo
   - Download the highest resolution version available

2. **Save the photo to the public folder:**
   ```bash
   # Save your photo as one of these:
   cp ~/Downloads/your-photo.jpg public/christophe-trefois.jpg
   # or
   cp ~/Downloads/your-photo.png public/christophe-trefois.png
   ```

3. **The photo will automatically appear on the About page**
   - The code is already set up to display `public/christophe-trefois.jpg` or `.png`
   - Recommended size: 400x400 pixels minimum
   - Format: JPG or PNG
